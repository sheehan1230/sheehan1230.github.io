Xuehai He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of California, Santa Cruz working with Xin Eric Wang. His research work mainly revolves around Multimodal Learning and Machine Learning. Before this, he was at University of California, San Diego working with Prof. Pengtao Xie. Xuehai began his research from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.
